## Project Overview

Student Discount is a dedicated navigation site compiling global student discount information and activation guides, helping students access products and services from brands like Spotify, Adobe, Apple, and Microsoft at lower prices. [The site supports multiple languages and covers discount policies and pricing information across countries and regions including the US, UK, SG, IN, and more.

## Key Features

- Aggregates discount percentages, eligible regions, and subscription prices for major brands' student offers, enabling quick side-by-side comparisons.
- Provides comprehensive step-by-step guides covering registration, verification, renewal, and refund processes, with official source links where possible to ensure verifiable information.
- Features a centralized Troubleshooting Center addressing common issues like failed student verification and regional restrictions, offering troubleshooting steps and solutions.
- Maintains a student benefits and community section, regularly updating educational tools, learning services, and shared perks.

## Who It's For

- Students seeking discounted subscriptions to streaming services like Spotify, Apple Music, Hulu, and Disney Plus.
- Students needing to purchase learning/creative software like Adobe Creative Cloud or Microsoft 365 while saving money.
- International students or those preparing to study abroad who want to systematically understand differences in student discounts, verification methods, and important considerations across countries/regions.
